Started in December 1986 by 20 young and skilled enthusiastic swimmers, today provides training to over three thousand children and adults every year. We believe in tapping talented children at young age through various programs and encourage adults to become healthier through this water sport.

We as a community,

  • Promote aquatic activities, such as swimming, water polo, diving, triathlon, and synchronized swimming.
  • Provide facilities to encourage youngsters in swimming and other aquatic activities.
  • Conduct coaching camps to enable swimmers to reach their competitive edge.
  • Represent athletes in the aquatic competitions conducted by various associations at District, State, National, and International levels.
